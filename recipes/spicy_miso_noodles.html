Spicy Miso Noodles

This is a quick and hearty one-pot recipe reminiscent of your favorite instant noodle recipe. A comforting recipe for rainy days or when you need a hot bowl of noodle soup when you're sick.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup frozen peas
  • 1/4 cup frozen corn
  • 1 oz crimini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 vegan beef bouillon cube
  • 2 tbsp of your favorite yellow miso
  • 1-2 tsp sambal (optional)
  • 4 oz medium tofu, sliced into 1 cm cubes
  • 2.5 cups water
  • 2 oz rice noodles

Directions

  1. Preheat a 2.5 quart stock pot on medium heat. When it comes up to temperature, add olive oil.
  2. Add sliced mushrooms and sautee for 2-3 minutes until the mushrooms wilt down.
  3. Add peas, corn, bouillon, and 1/2 cup water. Gently stir until the bouillon is mostly dissolved. Add in miso paste and stir until gently miso is mostly incorporated.
  4. Add in tofu cubes and remaining water. Bring heat up to high, cover the pot, and bring to a boil.
  5. Once it comes to a boil, add in rice noodles and cook according to package instructions.

Dietary accommodations

  • gluten-free
  • vegan
  • plant-based
